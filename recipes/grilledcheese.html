Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a perfect lunch in winter..

Enjoy it with some tomato soup too!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Brick of cheese

Steps

  1. Bring stove top to low-medium heat.
  2. Butter all slices of bread.
  3. Slice cheese evenly.
  4. Place sliced cheese onto non-buttered sides of bread.
  5. Make a sandwhich with buttered sides on the outside, place in pan.
  6. Cook for about 2 minutes each side or until brown and cheese is melted.
  7. Enjoy!
