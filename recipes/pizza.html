Pizza

Description

Having a party? Pizza is the only option.

Read below to make a wonderful, cheesy pizza!

Ingredients

  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Shredded cheese
  • Olives
  • Tomato paste
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Take your pizza crust and spread the tomato paste onto it evenly.
  2. Sprinkle two cups of shredded cheese onto the pizza.
  3. Cut your pepper into size of your choosing and sprinkle on.
  4. Take a quarter cup of olives and sprinkle on
  5. Place your pepperoni slices on the pizza a fingerwidths apart.
  6. Bake in oven at 425 for ~15 minutes until cheese is a golden brown.
  7. What are you waiting for? Enjoy!
