Rice Bowl

Description

This is my all time favourite dish, the rice bowl.

It is very easy to make and requires little time which is great when in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Ranch dressing
  • Peppers
  • Mixed vegetables
  1. Place chicken in oven at 375F for 25 minutes.
  2. Bring pot of water to boil with the rice, turn off oven and let sit.
  3. Place vegetables and peppers into pan with some oil and cook until slightly browned on edges.
  4. Check chicken after 25 minutes, if chickens internal temp is 165F we are done.
  5. Cut chicken and mix with the rice, along with the vegetables and peppers.
  6. Scoop onto your plate, add some ranch ontop and enjoy!
